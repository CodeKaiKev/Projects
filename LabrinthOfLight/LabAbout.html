    Labrynth is a group of individuals who are passionate about the mythology of Greek Gods. Our goal is to teach others about the wonders of Greek Mythology, through the likes of Zeus, Athena, Ares, Apollo, Hestia and Aphrodite. If you want to know more about each God, then please go to the Gallery and click on the respective God's image. This will take you to a website which will explain in detail about the God.
